Step-by-Step

How It Works

Our simple process connects investors with rural entrepreneurs for transparent, ethical growth

1 agriculture

Farmers Create Projects

Verified rural entrepreneurs list their detailed agricultural or artisan business plans for the community.

2 payments

Investors Fund Ventures

Choose from diverse bonds including crops, livestock, or handicrafts, and invest with as little as 5,000 BDT.

3 school

Training & Support

Admin provides continuous training to farmers and artisans, helping them improve skills and work effectively using their expertise.

4 manufacturing

Production & Monitoring

With guidance and resources in place, production begins while admin monitors progress and ensures quality standards are maintained.

5 storefront

Sales & Distribution

Admin collects and sells goods through online platform and local markets. Customers can also purchase directly from the platform.

6 trending_up

Profit Distribution

Generated profit is distributed fairly between investors as returns, farmers and artisans as income, and the platform for operational support.
